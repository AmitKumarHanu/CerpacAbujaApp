Welcome to the Federal Republic Of Nigeria

Federal Republic Of Nigeria is the official Government website for online visa applications which can be used by visa applicants.

This website can not be used to apply for a visa to visit countries other than Fedral Republic Of Nigeria.

Depending on which country you are in, you may also be able to pay your VISA fee through this website . To find out which additional services are available to you , Please Select Fees

You can find details of your nearest Nigeria Embassy or High Commission Nigeria office by using the "Foreign Mission" on this website.
