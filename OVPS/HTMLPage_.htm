Welcome to the Federal Republic Of Nigeria

Federal Republic Of Nigeria is the official Government website for online visa applications which can be used by visa applicants who are outside , the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man.

Please note that you cannot use this website to apply for a visa to visit other countries other than the Nigeria and Nigeria Overseas Territories.

Depending on which country you are in, you may also be able to pay your application fee and book an appointment at a visa application centre using Visa4Nigeria. To find out which services are available to you please select the Supported Countries link in the right hand navigation menu.

If you are not yet sure whether you need a visa to visit the Nigeria, please check the 'Do I Need a Nigeria Visa?' tool on the Nigeria Border Agency website.

You can find details of your nearest Nigeria Border Agency office by using the 'Where to apply' tool on the Nigeria Border Agency website.
