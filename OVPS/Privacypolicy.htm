1. This page explains how www , which is managed by Nigeria Immigration Services, uses any information you give to us, and the ways in which we protect your privacy.
2. Nigeria Immigration Services is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

The Data Protection Act
3. We will treat any personal information by which you can be identified (i.e. name, address, e-mail etc) in accordance with the provisions of the Data Protection Act of Federal Republic Of Nigeria.If you have any enquiry or concern regarding the processing of personal data please contact Us via the online feedback form.

4. Under the Data Protection Act, we have a legal duty to protect any information we collect from you. We use leading technologies and encryption software to safeguard your data, and keep strict security standards to prevent any unauthorised access to it.

5. If you have any queries about Data Protection not covered on the Data Protection section of the website - please email us at xxx@.......

What information do we collect?

6. The information we collect:

a. information contained in completed on-line forms; such as name, contact information, including email address, and information pertinent to processing your application for a visa or entry permit

b. information obtained through internet payments such as the value and currency and a unique payment reference number provided by our bankers

c. feedback (through email and an online feedback process)

d) bio-metric data.

Use of your data

7. We currently collect information via www in order to progress the entry clearance service; for accounting, billing and auditing (including checking credit or other payment cards); administrative and legal purposes; statistical analysis; developing and enhancing services provided on www. ; and facilitating security, immigration and entry procedures, in connection with your visa application.

8. We will only use personal information you provide for the purposes set out in paragraph 7 above. We will only hold your information for as long as necessary and will not pass it on to any other parties, except where required to carry out our duties and functions or as required by law. All employees and contractors that may have access to your personal data are obliged to respect the confidentiality of your personal data.

9. Your data may be disclosed to other Nigerian Government Departments, agencies, local authorities, foreign governments and other bodies for immigration purposes.

10. To complete any of our online forms, Nigeria Immigration Services will not use the personal information you provide us with for marketing purposes.

Internet payments

11. Payments made through the site are handled by WorldPay Ltd., who receive a reference number unique to your application, a brief description of the service provided (e.g. a six month visit Visa application) and the amount to be paid from the Nigeria Immigration Services .

12. Data provided during a transaction will only be used for recording your payment and for no other purposes.

Security

13. We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

14. We collect all the personal information you complete on electronic form(s). All the information is sent over a 128 bit secure encrypted connection.

Cookies

15. A cookie is a small piece of text stored on your computer, phone or whatever you use to surf the Internet. Cookies have many uses, but fundamentally they are used to store information about you on your computer. This site, www ,does not use cookies.
