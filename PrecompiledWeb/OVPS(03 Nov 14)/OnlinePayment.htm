New Visa Application - Before you start

To begin an online form you will require a valid email address. This is for security reasons and also so that we can contact you regarding your application.

We will also ask you to print your online visa application form as part of this process, therefore you will need the ability to print from your computer.

We also suggest that you have the following information to hand:

  • Your current Passport or Travel Document
  • An address in the Nigeria where you will be staying
  • Itinerary
  • Credit / Debit card / Your bank account details for making 'ON LINE' payment for VISA fee and other services.

  You can save your Visa application on any page where you see this image.

False documents
It is better to explain why you do not have a document than to submit a false document with an application. Your application may be refused and you may be banned from coming to the Nigeria for 10 years if you use a false document, lie or withhold relevant information.

You may also be banned if you have breached immigration laws in NIGERIA. Travellers to NIGERIA who produce a false travel document or passport to NIGERIA immigration authorities for themselves and/or their children are committing an offence and dealt as per Law of Federal Republic of NIGERIA.

Guidelines For Online Payment

As part of anti-fraud measures implemented to protect our valued customers, please note the following policy for making payments on the site:

1.Applicants first name and last name must match the first name and last name on the credit card used for payment.

Note: To qualify to use the cart to pay for family, the cardholder must also be an applicant in the cart.

Cards can be used once per month unless registered where applicable.

THE FOLLOWING USERS ARE REQUIRED TO REGISTER THEIR CARDS BEFORE MAKING PAYMENT ON THE SITE:

1. Applicants whose first names and last names do not exactly match the first and last names on their credit cards.

2. Cardholders making payments for others.

3. Applicants/cardholders who wish to make payments more than once a month on the site.

4.Applicants/cardholders such as travel agencies or businesses who wish to use the cart to make payment for more than 5 applicants at the same time.

TO ENSURE THAT YOUR PAYMENT IS PROPERLY PROCESSED, THE ADDRESS ON YOUR CARD STATEMENT MUST MATCH EXACTLY THE ADDRESS YOU PROVIDE ON THE PORTAL PAYMENT PAGE.
